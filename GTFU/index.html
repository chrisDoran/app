GTFU

Get The F**k Up

An app that turns your social media addiction, into motivation

Concept

Put on Your Sunday Clothes, There's lots of World out There

"The average person has five social media accounts and spends around 1 hour and 40 minutes browsing these networks every day, accounting for 28% of the total time spent on the internet."

-The Telegraph 2015

Recent reasearch from the University of Bergen, Norway, has begun to drawn clear parralles between social media usage and gambling addiction. This study also concluded that "People scoring high on narcissism tend to be more active on social network sites"

"Facebook 'addicts' have 10-20% smaller brain areas responsible for speech, memory, motor control, emotion, sensory and other information."

GTFU is all about getting people to get the f**k up from their computers and become motivated. Users of the app will have their access to certain chosen sites, such as facebook, limited or blocked completely, based on how much exercise they do. The app is less of a fitness app, and more of an AA program for people addicted to social media

Functionality

How it Works

Start Page

The first page that loads when the app is opened. Displays placeholders for information that will be filled when logged in.

Logged In

Once Logged in, username is displayed and personal data is retreived from a database. Functionality such as step tracking also becomes enabled.

Last Time Addded remains blank as we have yet to start tracking.

Step Tracking

When Start Tracking is pressed, the minutes earned towards facebook usage immediately start counting up, and adding to the total time remaining.

When Stop is pressed, this new information is transmitted to the online database for that user. Where it can be used for the extension.

Map

Once tracking begins, users can also view their route live on a handy map just beneath the pedometer. This data remains visible until the next Start Tracking action begins.

Chrome Extension

Once users log in to the Chrome Extension, they will see their earned time dispayed. If the user currently has facebook open, the time will instantly begin ticking down. Once it reaches Zero facebook will stop loading completely and the user will be forced to use the app to gain more time again.
